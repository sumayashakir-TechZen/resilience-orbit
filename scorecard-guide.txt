Resilience Orbit™ — Executive Scorecard (1‑Pager) Guide
Purpose: A single-page, board-ready snapshot to show resilience outcomes, risks, and next-step focus for this orbit (e.g., every 21 days). Keep it concise and decision-oriented.

Structure (in order):
1) Executive Snapshot (4 one‑liners)
   • Biggest Win — business outcome, not tech jargon.
   • Top Risk — what could go wrong next, with impact.
   • Next Orbit Focus — the #1 thing we’ll ship.
   • Decision Needed — Y/N and the ask (budget, people, policy).

2) KPIs (grouped)
   • Platform Health — Availability, Error Rate, p95 Latency.
   • Incident Readiness — MTTR, MTTD, Sev1/2 count.
   • Automation & Self‑Healing — Coverage %, Auto‑remediations #, Chaos Pass %.
   • Customer Impact — % Transactions Affected, Refunds/Comp % revenue.
   • Compliance & Risk — Critical Vulns #, Days to Remediate, Exceptions #.
   • Investment & ROI — Hours Saved, $ Avoided, Effort (FTE‑weeks).

RAG Defaults (tune as needed):
   • Availability: Green ≥ Target; Amber ≥ Target − 0.05 pp; else Red.
   • MTTR / MTTD: Green ≤ Target; Amber ≤ 120% of Target; else Red.
   • Automation Coverage: Green ≥ Target; Amber within 10 pp; else Red.
   • Chaos Pass Rate: Green ≥ 90%; Amber 70–89%; Red < 70%.
   • Transactions Affected & Refunds: Green ≤ Target; Amber ≤ 150% of Target; else Red.

Tips for Presenters:
   • Lead with outcomes and risks; park details in appendix.
   • Use one sentence per commentary; max 120 characters.
   • Do not change KPIs frequently — trends matter.
   • Keep owners on the page to show accountability.
   • If a KPI is Red twice in a row, it becomes a P1 for the next orbit.

How to Use the CSV:
   • Fill the SAMPLE once to align on thresholds and language.
   • Share the TEMPLATE each orbit; keep the format unchanged for comparability.
   • Convert to PDF for exec distribution (print-friendly, 1 page).
